Milkis

A Korean soft drink produced by Lotte Chilsung

The calm, cool, and collected beverage of my youth. Whenever I first pitch this drink to friends and family, it's always met with questioning looks [and maybe a litle bit of digust if your unfamiliar with anything like it]. But I always promise, it's absolutely delicious and refreshing. The original flavor is a tart and sweet carbonated yogurt soda that makes you feel a bit like a kid again. I can also assure that all of the other flavors that have been made are just as good. But they can never beat the OG.
Try it ASAP if you haven't already.
